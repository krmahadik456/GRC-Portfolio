## 🎯 Interview Talking Points

**Q: "Tell me about a risk assessment you performed."**
> "I conducted a comprehensive risk assessment for a mock fintech company. I identified 7 high-priority risks, scored them using a 5x5 risk matrix, and created a risk treatment plan with clear recommendations."

**Q: "How do you calculate risk?"**
> "I calculate risk by combining likelihood (1-5) and impact (1-5) to create a risk score. I also factor in existing controls and residual risk to prioritize recommendations."

**Q: "What frameworks did you use?"**
> "I aligned my risk assessment with ISO 27001 and NIST CSF methodologies."
